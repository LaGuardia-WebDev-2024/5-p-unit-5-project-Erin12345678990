You are invited to Viscountess Tsenira's grand ball

Dear name, the Viscountess has generously extended an invitation to you and a plus one. The date for the ball is to be set at the fifth of genosis and we will expect you to come. Do come dressed appropriately, or else you will not be let into the ball. If you cannot afford the proper attire, I suggest that you simply don't come instead of making a fool of yourself.

For our dear guest who has the attire to attend, there will be multitudes of festivites present.Besides from being able to dance in our beloved viscountess's beautiful ballroom, delicacies and drinks will be provided,delightful music,.
